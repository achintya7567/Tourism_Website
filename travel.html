COSTA RICA
"Pura Vida"- A Life with No Artificial Ingredients

Picturesque Volcanoes

5 active and over 60 dormant volcanoes grace the country providing hiking destination to tourists
Poas Volcano with a massive crater while a picturesque waterfall found near Arenal volcano
are the ones that attract tourists the most.

Delightful National Parks

National Geographic has called the Monteverde Cloud Forest Biological Reserve in Costa Rica
"THE JEWEL IN THE CROWN OF CLOUD FOREST RESERVES" while the Manuel Antonio National Park
was listed by Forbes among the world's 12 most beautiful national parks.

Serene Beaches

With such an expansive shoreline, the beaches of Costa Rica are diverse, with unique and varying personalities.
Conchal beach with white sand and coral reefs and Ostional beach famous for sea turtles are spots for eco-tourists
while those looking for adventure visit Tamarindo beach for surfing experience.
